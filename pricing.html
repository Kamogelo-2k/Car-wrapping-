FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a full wrap take?

+

Most full car wraps are completed within 3-5 business days, depending on the vehicle size and complexity. We'll provide an accurate timeline during your consultation.

Will the wrap damage my paint?

+

No, when properly installed and removed, vinyl wraps will not damage your paint. In fact, they protect your original paint from UV rays and minor scratches.

How long does a wrap last?

+

Premium wraps typically last 5-7 years with proper care. We provide a 5-year warranty on materials and installation, ensuring your investment is protected.

Can I wash my wrapped vehicle?

+

Yes! You can wash your wrapped vehicle normally. We recommend hand washing with mild soap and avoiding high-pressure washers on edges. We provide detailed care instructions with every installation.

Do you offer financing options?

+

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans and financing options for qualifying customers. Contact us to discuss available payment options.
