About WrapPro

Excellence in vehicle transformation since 2014

Our Story

Driven by Passion, Built on Excellence

WrapPro was founded in 2014 with a simple mission: to transform vehicles and protect paint with the highest quality materials and craftsmanship. What started as a small workshop in Johannesburg has grown into South Africa's premier vehicle wrapping and branding company.

Our journey began when our founder, frustrated with the lack of quality wrapping services in South Africa, decided to set a new standard. We invested in premium materials, trained our team to perfection, and built relationships with leading vinyl manufacturers worldwide.

Today, we've wrapped over 5,000 vehicles, from luxury sports cars to commercial fleets. Our commitment to excellence has earned us the trust of car enthusiasts, businesses, and automotive dealers across the country.
